About Us

Image Compressor is a free online tool designed to help users reduce image file sizes quickly and safely. Our goal is to provide simple, fast, and privacy-focused tools that anyone can use without technical knowledge.

Unlike many online tools, our image compression runs entirely inside your browser. This means your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

In addition to our own tools, we also guide users to trusted third-party services for PDF and file conversions. These links are clearly marked, and users are informed whenever they leave our website.

This website is built using modern web technologies and hosted on a secure platform. We are committed to keeping the site fast, lightweight, and easy to use.

If you have any feedback or suggestions, feel free to contact us. We plan to expand this website in the future with more useful tools.
